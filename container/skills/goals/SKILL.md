# 2026 Goals

**Theme:** Nothing changes if nothing changes. Year of radical action.

## The 6 Hard Goals

### 1. Career: New Job, Making Impact
- **Target:** Chief of Staff / Head of Ops at AI company
- **Comp:** $400K base + ~$1M/year equity + $100K sign-on + 15-20% bonus
- **Status:** On sabbatical, pipeline active, focus on conversion
- **Key results:** 5+ intro convos, 3+ formal processes, 2 competitive offers, start by May 2026
- **Daily commitment:** One outreach or interview prep action every day
- **Weekly rhythm:** Mon (review pipeline), Wed (follow-ups), Fri (deep research on 1 company), Sun (check-in)

### 2. Body: 165 lbs, Under 15% Body Fat
- **Phase 1 — Lean Bulk (Feb-Aug):** 2,850 cal/day, 200g protein, accept temporary fat gain
- **Phase 2 — Cut (Aug-Nov):** 2,000-2,200 cal/day, maintain protein, add cardio
- **Phase 3 — Maintain (Nov+):** Find maintenance calories
- **DEXA checkpoints:** August + November
- **Key results:** Hit daily calories, hit protein, DEXA-verified progress
- **Note:** Nut allergy — never suggest nut-containing foods

### 3. Face: 10/10 Face Card
- **Key outcomes:** Even skin, less hollow under-eyes, sharper jawline, lean face
- **Daily:** AM/PM skincare, lymphatic drainage, jaw exercises, LED mask 5x/week
- **Quarterly treatments:** Chemical peels (Jan/Apr/Jul/Oct), EZ Gel (Feb/May/Aug/Nov)
- **Annual:** Fraxel (Nov/Dec), KTP Laser
- **Key results:** Zero missed skincare days, LED 5x/week, all treatments on schedule

### 4. Financial: $750K Altruist Target
- **Current position:** ~$995K liquid, ~$1.12M net worth
- **Key results:** $750K in Altruist by Dec 31, AMPL concentration below 25%, monthly spending check-ins
- **The bigger picture:** $2M liquid unlocks a 6-month creative sabbatical (the real dream)

### 5. Awai: 50 Active Community Members
- **Key results:** First 2026 event (Q2), chef partner outreach, monthly/bimonthly cadence, 50 members by Q4

### 6. Self: Prioritize Myself Above All
- **The meta-goal that makes everything else work**
- **Daily:** Bed by 9pm, wake 5:30am, manifestation journaling, visualization during LED mask
- **Weekly:** Sunday check-in, one new experience, one social connection
- **Pattern-breaking:** Notice over-giving, boundary-sliding, guilt about self-investment

## How to Coach

When Shintaro asks for a check-in, goal focus, or coaching:

1. **Ask how he's feeling first** — don't jump to metrics
2. **Be direct and warm** — like a sharp friend who genuinely cares
3. **Push him on patterns:** avoiding hard conversations, doing everything himself, philosophical mode without action
4. **Celebrate compound progress** — small wins matter
5. **Reference his own words:** "Nothing changes if nothing changes"
6. **Don't be productivity-brained** — he's a human, not a project

## Weekly Check-in Prompts (Sunday Evening)

1. What did I do this week that I'm proud of?
2. What did I avoid or procrastinate on?
3. Where did I break a pattern vs. fall into one?
4. Career: What moved forward? What's the one thing for next week?
5. Body: How was nutrition adherence? Did I hit the gym?
6. Face: Any missed skincare days or treatments?
7. Financial: Am I spending intentionally?
8. Creative/Community: Did I do anything for Awai or photography?
9. What's the one thing that would make next week great?
